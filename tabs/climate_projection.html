Understanding CLIRAM, CERAM & CMIP6

CLIRAM

Shows future averages — how "normal" months will change (2031-2060).

CERAM

Shows future extremes — how "worst days" will worsen (2051-2080).

CMIP6

Global climate model framework. CLIRAM and CERAM are localized versions.

Impact Statements

💧 RX1day Increase → Rain-Induced Landslides (RIL)

Projections indicate a consistent increase in RX1day. For Mountain Province's mountainous terrain, this directly elevates the risk of rain-induced landslides.

🌊 RX5day Increase → Riverine Flooding

Prolonged heavy rainfall increases soil saturation and river discharge, leading to riverine flooding.

🌡️ TXx Increase → Heat Health Emergency

Extreme daytime temperatures are projected to increase by 2.6–3.1°C. Heat stroke risks will rise significantly.
